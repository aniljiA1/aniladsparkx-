# General FAQ

**Q: How do I change my email address on file?**
A: Go to Account Settings > Profile > Email, enter the new address, and confirm via the verification link sent to it.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, under Account Settings > Data Export, you can request a full export as CSV or JSON. Exports are emailed within 24 hours.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to Billing > Subscription > Cancel Plan. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle; no partial refunds are issued for monthly plans.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Yes, available on iOS and Android under the same product name.

**Q: How do I add team members?**
A: Account Settings > Team > Invite Member. Pro plans allow up to 10 seats; Enterprise is unlimited.
