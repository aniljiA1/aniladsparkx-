# Password Reset Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how customers can reset their account password if they are locked out or have forgotten their credentials.

## Steps to Reset Password
1. Navigate to the login page and click "Forgot Password".
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link. The link expires after 30 minutes.
4. Click the link and enter a new password. Passwords must be at least 10 characters and include one number and one symbol.
5. Confirm the new password and log in.

## Common Issues
- **Reset email not received**: Check spam/junk folder. Confirm the email address is correct. Emails can take up to 5 minutes to arrive.
- **Reset link expired**: Request a new reset link; old links cannot be reused.
- **Account locked after 5 failed attempts**: Accounts are automatically locked for 15 minutes after 5 failed login attempts as a security measure. Wait 15 minutes or contact support to unlock immediately.

## Security Notes
We never ask for your password via email or chat. If you receive such a request, report it as phishing immediately.
