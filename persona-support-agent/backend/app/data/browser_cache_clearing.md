# Clearing Browser Cache and Cookies

Stale cache and cookies are the most common cause of pages not loading correctly or showing outdated UI elements.

## Chrome
1. Settings > Privacy and Security > Clear Browsing Data.
2. Select "Cookies and site data" and "Cached images and files".
3. Choose time range "All time" and click Clear Data.

## Firefox
1. Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data > Clear Data.

## Safari
1. Preferences > Advanced > Show Develop menu, then Develop > Empty Caches.

## Still Not Loading?
If the issue persists after clearing cache:
- Try an incognito/private window to rule out extensions.
- Check our status page for ongoing incidents.
- If the page still fails to load, this indicates a server-side issue and should be escalated.
