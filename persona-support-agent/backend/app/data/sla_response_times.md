# Support SLA and Response Times

## Plan-Based SLAs
| Plan | First Response | Resolution Target |
|------|----------------|--------------------|
| Free | 48 hours | Best effort |
| Pro | 8 business hours | 2 business days |
| Enterprise | 1 hour | 4 hours (Sev1), 1 business day (Sev2/3) |

## Severity Definitions
- **Sev1 (Critical)**: Full outage or data loss impacting production.
- **Sev2 (High)**: Major feature unusable, no workaround.
- **Sev3 (Normal)**: Minor bug or question, workaround exists.

## Operational Impact Reporting
Enterprise customers receive a status update every 60 minutes during a Sev1 incident, including estimated resolution time and root cause once known.
