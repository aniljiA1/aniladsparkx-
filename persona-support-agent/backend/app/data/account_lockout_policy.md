# Account Lockout Policy

## Trigger Conditions
An account is automatically locked when:
- 5 consecutive failed login attempts occur within 10 minutes.
- Suspicious login activity is detected from an unrecognized country.
- A user reports their account as compromised.

## Lockout Duration
- Standard lockout: 15 minutes, auto-unlocks.
- Security-flagged lockout (suspicious activity): requires manual identity verification by support before unlock.

## Unlocking Your Account
1. Wait for the standard 15 minute cooldown, or
2. Use the "Unlock Account" link sent via email, or
3. Contact support with your account email and a government ID for security-flagged lockouts.
