BILLING AND REFUND POLICY

1. BILLING CYCLE
Customers are billed monthly on the anniversary of their signup date. Invoices are generated automatically and emailed as PDF receipts.

2. DUPLICATE CHARGES
Duplicate charges can occur due to payment gateway retries during network timeouts. If you notice a duplicate charge:
- Duplicate charges are automatically detected by our reconciliation system within 24-48 hours and refunded automatically in most cases.
- If not auto-refunded within 3 business days, this should be escalated to the billing team for manual review, since it involves direct financial dispute.

3. REFUND ELIGIBILITY
- Monthly subscriptions: refundable within 7 days of charge if usage was minimal.
- Annual subscriptions: prorated refund available within 30 days.
- Add-on purchases (storage, seats): non-refundable once provisioned.

4. FAILED PAYMENTS
If a payment fails, the account enters a 7-day grace period before service is suspended. Customers receive 3 email reminders during this period.

5. DISPUTES AND CHARGEBACKS
Any billing dispute, chargeback, or legal inquiry must be routed to a human billing specialist; automated systems cannot issue manual refunds or discuss legal liability.
